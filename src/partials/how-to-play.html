How to Play

  • Swap adjacent candies to create a row or column of three or more identical ones.

  • Match four or more candies to create special candies with powerful effects.

  • Remove obstacles like jelly, chocolate, and more by matching nearby candies.

  • Complete levels by collecting specific candy types or achieving score targets.

  • Utilize special boosters to help clear challenging levels.

  • Progress through the map by completing levels and unlocking new challenges.
